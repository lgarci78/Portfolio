      Engage in thoughtful turn-based battles on a grid-based battlefield. Each turn, you must plan and execute strategic movements and mergers to achieve high scores...

    • Grid Manipulation: Enjoy the ability to cycle individual rows and columns, offering a fresh perspective on solving the 2048 puzzle.
    • Solid Blocks: Introducing immovable Black Blocks that pose an intriguing challenge as you strategize your moves.
    • Solid Block Transformation: Merge two normal blocks adjacent to a Solid Block to convert it back into a regular block. The key is to match their combined value to the Solid Block's value.
    • Color Theme Customization: Personalize your gaming experience by selecting different color themes from the dropdown at the bottom of the screen.
