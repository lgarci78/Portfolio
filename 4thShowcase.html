      In a world ruled by robots, the premier entertainment source is "Magnet Mania", an arena where contestants can play an ELECTRIFYING game to win rewards. You must work together with your partner to destroy as many pucks as possible. "Good luck, and BOO BAP BEEP BEEW"!

    • Magnetism Mechanics: The core mechanic of the game revolves around magnetism. Pucks with the same charge are attracted to the player, while pucks with the opposite charge are repelled. This creates a dynamic environment where players must navigate and manipulate the pucks to their advantage.
    • Cooperative Gameplay: The game promotes teamwork, as both players need to communicate and coordinate their actions to create effective puck collisions. This cooperative aspect adds depth and strategy to the gameplay.
    • Win/Lose Conditions: The primary objective is to accumulate points by colliding pucks. However, players also need to avoid being touched by pucks of the opposite charge. If a player is touched, they lose points or the game might end prematurely, adding an element of risk.
